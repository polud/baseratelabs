LottoLab Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

The short version. Everything you log in LottoLab stays on your phone: the draws you played, the wheels you built, what you spent and won. We have no accounts and no logins, we can't see your play history, we don't run ads, we don't track you across apps, and we never sell data. The only personal-ish thing that leaves your device is a notification token, and only if you turn notifications on.

Who we are

LottoLab is published by Base Rate Labs ("we", "us"). LottoLab is an analytics and education app for Lotto Max. It does not sell lottery tickets, is not affiliated with or endorsed by Loto-Québec, the Interprovincial Lottery Corporation, or any lottery operator, and it does not predict winning numbers.

What stays on your device

This data is written to your phone's local app storage. It is never uploaded to us, and we cannot read it. If you uninstall the app, it is gone.

  • Draws you logged and confirmed: the wheels and their lines, what you spent, what you won, and whether you played or skipped.
  • Your settings: spending limit, notification preferences.
  • App state: whether you've seen the intro, and the seed used to keep your free wheel stable for a draw.

What leaves your device

DataWhenWhy
A notification token (a random identifier for your app install) and your platform (Android or iOS) Only while "Jackpot alerts" is on in Settings So we can send you a notification when the jackpot climbs. Turning the setting off deletes the token from our database.
Purchase and subscription status Only if you buy Premium To unlock Premium features and restore them on your devices. Payment itself is handled by the app store. We never see your card or billing details.
Your IP address and standard request data Whenever the app loads draw results An unavoidable part of any internet request. It appears in our hosting provider's server logs and is not used to profile you.

Lottery draw results are public information that we fetch from our server. Requesting them tells us nothing about you beyond the request itself.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, no logins, no email collection.
  • No advertising and no ad identifiers.
  • No analytics or behavioural tracking, in this app or across other apps.
  • No selling, renting or trading of personal information, ever.
  • No access to your contacts, location, photos, camera or microphone.
  • No automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you.

Keeping and deleting your data

  • Your play log: lives only on your phone. Delete it anytime in Settings → Clear my logged draws, or by uninstalling the app.
  • Notification token: deleted from our database as soon as you turn off "Jackpot alerts". We also drop tokens that the push service reports as no longer valid. We keep a token only while alerts are on.
  • Purchase records: retained by RevenueCat and the app store for as long as needed to honour your subscription and meet their legal and accounting obligations.
  • Server logs: kept for a short period by our hosting provider for security and reliability.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or port the personal information we hold about you, to withdraw consent, and to lodge a complaint with your privacy regulator. These rights come from laws such as Québec's Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector (Law 25), Canada's PIPEDA, and the GDPR.

In practice we hold very little: if you have notifications on, we hold a notification token; if you bought Premium, our subscription provider holds your purchase record. Most of your data never reaches us and you can delete it yourself on your phone. To exercise any right, write to us at the address at the end of this policy and we will respond within the time limit the applicable law requires (30 days under Law 25 and PIPEDA).

Québec residents may contact the Commission d'accès à l'information du Québec.

Children

LottoLab is intended for adults of legal lottery age in their jurisdiction (18+ in Québec). It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If we change this policy we will update the date at the top and, for anything significant, tell you in the app before the change takes effect.
